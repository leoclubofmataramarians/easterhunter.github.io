The Golden Egg Protocol

The Golden Egg has been stolen.
Not by chance… but by design.

Clues have been scattered across our digital world — hidden in places you scroll past every day.

Some are obvious.
Some are not.

If you wish to find the treasure, you must observe, think, and connect.

Your first clue:
“The hunt begins where we speak the most.”

⏱ You have limited time.

Level 2 — Disappearing Clue

Good. You’ve taken your first step.

But this hunt is not that simple.
Some clues… do not stay forever.

If you hesitate, you may miss them.
Look where things appear… and disappear.

Level 3 — Seeing VS Looking

You’re progressing… but many fail here.

Because they look… but they do not see.

The answer is right in front of you —
But only if you take a closer look.

⚠️ Most players fail at Level 3.

Level 4 — The Past Holds Truth

You’re closer than most.

But the next clue is not new.
It was already placed… before you even started.

The past holds what you seek.
Go back. Look again.

👁‍🗨 Not all clues are helpful.

Level 5 — Look Below

You’re almost there.

But this time…
Stop looking above.

The answer lies below.
Not everything important is highlighted.

Final Level — The Truth

You’ve done well.
Few make it this far.

Now… everything comes together.
You have collected:

GOLDEN — EASTER — EGG — LIGHT — HOPE

But not all belong to the treasure.
Remove what describes the moment…
Keep only what describes what was stolen.
